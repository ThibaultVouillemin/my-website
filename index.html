Our idea :

Our idea is to create a restaurant where the atmosphere is as important as the dishes offered. The goal is to arrive in a warm atmosphere where everything is out of the ordinary and the dishes are only dishes to share.

How to choose?

This restaurant is different from the others, the atmosphere, the interior decoration, the music and the good humor of the waiters encourage sharing. That's why all the dishes are to be shared, it's an experience, a mixture of flavors, a mixture of cultures.

Thibault Vouillemin (thibault.vouillemin@ieseg.fr)

Why call on us?

Call on the restaurant for a dinner, a cocktail, an aperitif, with your family, your friends, your other half. Given, the good mood that is felt in the restaurant, you can go there to celebrate something or cheer up.
